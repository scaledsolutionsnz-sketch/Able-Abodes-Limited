Our Story

From Homeless
to Managing Director

Able Abodes was founded by Cyndee Elder — a Dunedin mum who experienced homelessness and refused to let others go through the same. With no housing background, she built a team of passionate local tradespeople and turned a vision into real homes.

Today, Able Abodes is a locally owned business with big plans: a 16-lot development at Karitāne, rent-to-own programmes, and a mission to make homeownership achievable for every New Zealander.
