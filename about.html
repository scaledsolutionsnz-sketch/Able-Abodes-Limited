Able Abodes is a Dunedin-based company with a deeply personal mission — to make quality homeownership achievable for every New Zealander, regardless of income, background, or circumstance.

Cyndee Elder — Managing Director, Able Abodes
Our Founder

From Emergency Accommodation to Managing Director

In 2016, Cyndee Elder found herself in emergency accommodation after leaving an abusive relationship. Eight weeks in transitional housing, with an 18-month waiting list for social housing, was the moment that sparked a mission.

"I had a vision of what I wanted to do and I just gunned it."

With no background in housing, construction, or development — but with an extraordinary ability to bring people together — Cyndee assembled a skilled team of local Dunedin tradespeople: an architect, builder, joiner, electrician, and plumber. All united by a shared belief that they could do something meaningful.

Today, Able Abodes is a growing company with real homes on real sites, a community development underway at Karitāne, and a rent-to-own programme opening doors for people who thought homeownership was out of reach.

Why We Do What We Do

New Zealand's housing crisis is real. Able Abodes was created to offer a genuine alternative — not just to talk about the problem, but to build the solution, one home at a time.
