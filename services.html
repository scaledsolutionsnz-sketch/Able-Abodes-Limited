Design & Build
Service 01

Architecturally Designed Tiny Homes

Our flagship 54m² homes are designed by qualified architects using passive design and universal principles. Every square metre is fully optimised for comfortable, functional, everyday living.

Built off-site at our Dunedin yard using locally sourced materials and a skilled team of local contractors, your home is ready to place on any approved site in New Zealand.

  • 6m x 9m footprint — smart use of every metre
  • SIP panel construction for strength and insulation
  • High-quality ventilation, heating, and data systems
  • Multi-proof building consent — fully code compliant
  • Custom finishes and colour selections
  • One bedroom, bathroom-laundry, kitchen, and deck

Accessible
Service 02

Accessible & Disability Housing

Every Able Abode is available in an accessible configuration — designed with independence, dignity, and comfort in mind. We don't want it to look clinical. We want it to feel like home.

Our accessible layout includes a wet floor bathroom with seat bench, wider doorways, and thoughtful placement of fixtures to suit all mobility needs.

  • Wet floor bathroom with seat bench option
  • Wider doorways and turning radius clearances
  • Accessible kitchen layout with lower benches
  • Universal design — suits all ages and abilities
  • Indoor-outdoor flow with ramped deck access

Rent-to-Own
Service 03

Rent-to-Own Homeownership

Our rent-to-own programme is designed for people who are ready for a home but not yet ready for a traditional mortgage. Through affordable weekly payments, you build equity from day one.

That equity can then be used to secure lending for land or your next home — creating a genuine pathway to long-term homeownership without needing a large deposit upfront.

  • Affordable weekly payment structure
  • Equity builds from your first payment
  • Use equity to secure future lending
  • Suitable for those on housing waiting lists
  • Designed with housing hardship in mind

Community
Service 04

Karitāne Community Development

Able Abodes has purchased land at Karitāne, north of Dunedin, and is developing it into sixteen 400m² sections available as lease lots for those who buy or rent-to-own an Able Abode.

The vision is to create pockets of affordable community housing across New Zealand — neighbourhoods where people own their home and belong to a community that looks after each other.

  • 16 leased lots at Karitāne, north Dunedin
  • 400m² sections — room to breathe
  • Beautiful coastal location north of Dunedin
  • Community-first design philosophy
  • Future developments planned across NZ
